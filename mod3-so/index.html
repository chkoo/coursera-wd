Chicken

Chicken is the most common type of poultry in the world. In developed countries, chickens are usually subject to intensive farming methods.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle. Beef can be harvested from bulls, cows, heifers or steers. Its acceptability as a food source varies in different parts of the world.

Fish

Fish are consumed as food by many species, including humans. It has been an important source of protein and other nutrients for humans throughout recorded history.
